# 1. The main problem with your current structure

Current:
Graphic Design Solutions ❌ (sounds like a specialist design studio)
Web Development and Design ✅ (your core service)
Domain setup & Webhosting ⚠️ (technical add-on, good)
Online Digital Marketing & SEO ⚠️ (broad + separate discipline)
Issue:

One service sounds like a design agency, the rest sound like a web developer offering extras.

That creates confusion about what you actually are.

## 2. The key fix: unify everything under a “web services” identity

You don’t need to become a “branding/design studio.”

You are:

A web developer who also provides practical supporting services.

So your service names should reflect supporting roles around websites, not separate agencies inside your business.

## 3. Recommended restructuring (clean + professional)

Here’s a much more aligned version:

1. Website Development & Design (MAIN SERVICE)
    - This is your core.

    - Rename stays close:
        - Website Development & Design

        - Web Design & Development (or slightly cleaner)

2. Website Graphics & Visual Support (replace Graphic Design Solutions)
    - This is where your current “graphic design” fits.

    - Better names:
        - Website Graphics & Visual Support
        - Web Visual Design Support
        - Practical Website Design Assets

        👉 This makes it clear it’s supporting, not high-end design agency work

3. Domain, Hosting & Website Setup
    - This one is good, just clean formatting:

    - Domain Registration & Hosting Setup
      or
    - Website Setup & Hosting Management

4. SEO & Digital Marketing Basics

    Important wording change here:

    Instead of “Online Digital Marketing & SEO” (too broad), use:

    SEO & Website Marketing Basics
    or
    Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Website Visibility

    👉 This avoids sounding like a full marketing agency

## 4. Why this structure works better

Now everything aligns under one identity:

You become:

“A web developer offering complete website setup + practical support services”

Not:

“A mixed agency offering design, dev, marketing, and branding”

That difference matters a lot in trust and pricing expectations.

## 5. How your services now read (client perception)

Before:

“This person is trying to do everything (design agency vibes)”

After:

“This is a developer who can also handle the basics so I don’t need multiple suppliers”

That second perception is exactly what you want.

## 6. What I strongly recommend changing immediately

Rename this:

❌ Graphic Design Solutions
➡️ Website Graphics & Visual Support

This is the single most important fix in your list.

## 7. Optional improvement (if you want to level up your site)

You can group services like this:

- Web Services
    - Web Design & Development
    - Website Setup (Domain & Hosting)
- Website Growth
    - SEO & Visibility
- Visual Support
    - Website Graphics & Visual Support

This makes your site feel more structured and intentional.

## 8. One-line positioning for your services page

You can use this at the top of your services section:

    “Complete website solutions including development, setup, and practical design support for small businesses.”
